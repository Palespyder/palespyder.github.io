FIND WITH ME

Portfolio - Educational Study of In-Game Water Mechanics and Rendering

I wanted to find out how realistic water planes were created. This lead me down a rabbit hole of using gerstner waves in a material shader and procedural mesh manipulation to get this result.

I worked all all aspects of this project as it was for educational purposes. I used multiple academic sources for the wave generation, using fast fourier transforms, and other aspects of the project. I also researched deeply into creating shaders in Unreal Engine, as well as rendering on the GPU.


The majority of the work on the procedural mesh and calculations were done in C++, while most of the visual aspects, texturing, and shader work was done in Blueprints.
